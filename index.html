About Me

My name is Umang Vaish, and I am a student residing in Princeton, New Jersey. I currently go to Montgomery High School. I like to design websites, practice Tae Kwon Do, surf the internet and play games. Oh, and I also like to code. I have been the Programming captain of F.I.R.S.T. Team 1403 for the past two years, and I have coded the robot in a popular program called LabVIEW. Now that I am graduating, I will be studying engineering in college.

If you want a more complete bio, visit the About me page!

My Work

As you've probably already seen, I have some experience creating websites. My first client was Dushahra, a non-profit cultural organization.

You can see more images of my work for Dushahra and other organizations in my Portfolio.
